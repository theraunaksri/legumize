Have A Questions?

1 Why there is required an agreement?
The main reason that you need a contract is to protect your rights when you enter an agreement with another party.
2 Why sales deed is required?
A property sale deed is necessary since it is a legal document drawn on non-judicial stamp paper according to the state's stamp duty value. This document serves as proof of a property sale or purchase between two parties in front of two adult witnesses. A description of the property is included in a sale deed.
3 what is power of attorney?
the right to act as the representative of somebody in business or financial matters; a document that gives somebody this right
4 how to get marriage certificate?
Marriage registration Application forms. Affidavit in the prescribed format. Age proof of the Groom and Bride – Birth certificate or Mark Sheet. Address Proof – Ration card, Voter ID, Driving licence, Passport.
5 what is E-stamp?
e Stamping or electronic stamping is a digital process that enables users to pay non-judicial stamp duty to the government. It is an online approach that simplifies stamp duty payment and makes the process convenient for users.
